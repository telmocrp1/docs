Welcome to the Find Contacts API documentation. Our tool enables you to efficiently discover professional contacts by querying company domains with specific criteria.

<Card title="Key Features" icon="stars">

  - Retrieve matching employee profiles
  - Automatic email enrichment for discovered contacts
</Card>

With Find Contacts, you can streamline your outreach efforts, enhance your lead generation process, and build targeted professional networks with ease.
